Welcome to Conqueror 1066, an action-adventure game where history, strategy, and combat converge in the year 1066. You are a Norman knight, an elite soldier in William the Conqueror's army, tasked with the monumental mission of conquering England. Your journey will take you through lush countrysides, besieged fortresses, and into the heart of epic battles. Your decisions will shape the future of England.

Missions Overview

1. The Beaches of Pevensey

Objective: Secure a foothold on the English shore.
Challenges: Navigate the coastline defenses, overcome the local militia.
Key Decisions: Choose to launch a direct assault or use diplomacy to sway the local lords.

2. The March Inland

Objective: Move your forces inland, ensuring safe passage for supplies and reinforcements.
Challenges: Ambushes by Saxon warriors, navigating the difficult terrain.
Key Decisions: Opt for stealthy movement at night or fortify your caravan and move by day.

3. The Siege of York

Objective: Lay siege to the city of York, a strategic Viking stronghold.
Challenges: Breaching the city's defenses, managing siege equipment.
Key Decisions: Negotiate with the Vikings for a peaceful surrender or storm the city by force.

4. The Battle of Stamford Bridge

Objective: Intercept and defeat the Viking army returning from their raid into Mercia.
Challenges: Quick maneuvering to catch the Vikings by surprise, dealing with the harsh weather conditions.
Key Decisions: Launch a full-frontal attack or divide your forces for a pincer movement.

5. The Battle of Hastings

Objective: The final showdown against the Saxon army led by King Harold Godwinson.
Challenges: Overcoming the Saxon shield wall, adapting to the dynamic battlefield.
Key Decisions: Use your cavalry effectively, decide when to commit your reserves.

Controls

Movement

  • WASD: Move your character
  • Shift: Sprint
  • Space: Jump / Climb

Combat

  • Left Mouse Button (LMB): Basic attack
  • Right Mouse Button (RMB): Block / Parry
  • Middle Mouse Button (MMB): Special ability (varies by weapon)
  • Q: Quick dodge
  • E: Interact / Use item

Strategy

  • Tab: Open the map and objectives
  • F: Signal troops to follow / hold position
  • G: Use the horn to call for horse
  • C: Crouch / Stealth mode

Miscellaneous

  • I: Inventory
  • M: Open missions menu
  • Esc: Pause menu

Tips for Success

  • Explore Thoroughly: The English countryside is filled with hidden paths, resources, and allies willing to aid your cause.
  • Upgrade Your Equipment: Loot fallen enemies and visit blacksmiths to enhance your armor and weapons.
  • Choose Wisely: Your decisions have lasting effects on the game's world and its inhabitants. Think carefully about the consequences.
  • Master Combat: Practice blocking, dodging, and using your special abilities. Knowing when to attack and when to defend can turn the tide of battle.

Embark on your journey to change the course of history in Conqueror 1066. Your valor and strategic acumen will determine the fate of England. Godspeed, knight.
